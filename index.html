Hello People! Welcome to the New York city!

New York is a really fun place. There are lots of activities in the seasons. Some activities in the winter are ice skating, and skiing. In addition, in the summer you can have nice cold ice cream, play lots of sports, and most of all, ENJOY A BREAK FROM SCHOOL. In the spring, you can go to a place in Manhattan called Central Park, when all of the leaves are changing colors. It is a nice sight. Overall, New York ROCKS!

Great Places to visit in NYC

  • The Statue of Liberty

    The Statue of Liberty is a monument that the French created for 100 years of independence in the American Revolutionary war. The Statue of Liberty lies in Liberty island.

  • Central Park

    Central Park, is a huge park made by Robert Moses. It was a place where citizens could get fresh air, and relax.

  • Yankee Stadium

    Yankee Stadium is where people play baseball, it is a huge stadium with tons of fans. If you catch a ball, you can keep it.
